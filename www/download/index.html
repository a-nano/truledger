The first two columns in the table above are installers (except for the FreeBSD row). The third and fourth columns are compressed raw binaries. If you're running Truledger as a client on your PC, you probably want an installer. If you're running Truledger as a server, you probably want a raw binary.

For Windows, you need to do three installs, vcredist, OpenSSL, and Truledger. Use the installers in the column for your machine's architecture (32-bit or 64-bit).
